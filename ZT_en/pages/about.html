About POSTALONG

Who We Are

Postalong utilizes our global connectivity through telecommunications, computers and smart devices to lower the transaction costs of mobility (travel), purchase (goods), and resolution (payments). This will create a much more efficient and timely system which utilizes people’s current resources, knowledge, advice and information. This is the “Shared Economy” model. Trust is the main factor of efficient commerce. By using Postalong, Members have a robust, reliable and trustworthy system for mutual assistance and benefits. Postalong Technology Inc. is committed to making a better world through increased economic opportunity, prosperity, trust and advanced technologies.

On-Demand Shared Services Platform

POSTALONG is a mobile and web-based platform for people to post and respond to requests for a wide variety of goods and services in a peer-to-peer exchange. This “crowd-sourced, supply and demand” business model takes advantage of people’s natural movements (mobility) with an easy to use mobile application called “Postalong” (互递HuDi in Chinese). People broadcast their service request (“problem”) to our Postalong Members (community) and any Member can respond with a solution. A Postalong Member can ask for help or provide help to another Member. A two-way interactive exchange then occurs between the Members, all recorded and tracked by Postalong in real-time. The Postalong Platform can automatically match requests with solutions … or … each Member can search anytime for an opportunity to help another Member. People can purchase goods, have their goods transported, or shop online. “Carriers” are Postalong Members that will transport goods for another Member. A Member will pay another Member for this service. Postalong can provide information through the entire transaction to assist the Members in the fulfilling of the request, facilitate payment, and give advice if requested.

The Future

We simplify the transaction, optimize unused resources, and reduce costs for individuals, our communities, and ultimately improve our environment. As people help us grow, Postalong will be able to add additional applications and services utilizing the same trusted platform and mobile app. Postalong will also work closely with other trusted strategic business partners to extend and expand our reach so that our Members will have more ways to satisfy their demands and earn money. Postalong will be the sum of its Members … a new powerful voice that can bring continuous positive change to the world … creating safer, healthier communities and opening up prosperous opportunities to millions and millions of people globally.
